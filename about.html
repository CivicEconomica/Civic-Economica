About Me

Hello! My name is Ellie and I am a year 13 A-level student with a strong interest in economics, politics and public policy. I created Civic Economica as a personal initiative to explore major political and economic issues through independent research and critical analysis.

Why This Website?

My goal is to publish one in-depth article each week on topics such as UK fiscal policy, global trade, social inequality and current affairs. This platform allows me to develop my ideas, strengthen my writing, and build a portfolio for university applications, particularly for courses in Economics, PPE and related disciplines.

Academic Interests

  • Macroeconomics & Fiscal Policy
  • International Relations
  • Public Policy Analysis

UCAS Profile

I am applying to study PPE and Politics and International Relations at university. This blog forms part of my broader academic engagement, alongside wider reading, essay competitions and participation in economics societies.
